#### Latihan 1: Pertidaksamaan Linear

1. Selesaikan $2x - 5 > 9$.
   **Jawab:** $$
   \begin{aligned}
   2x - 5 &> 9 \\
   2x &> 9 + 5 \\
   2x &> 14 \\
   x &> \frac{14}{2} \\
   x &> 7
   \end{aligned}
   $$
2. Selesaikan $3x + 4 \leq 19$.
   **Jawab:** $$
   \begin{aligned}
	 3x + 4 &\leq 19 \\
	 3x &\leq 19 - 4 \\
	 3x &\leq 15 \\
	 x &\leq \frac{15}{3} \\
	 x \leq 5
   \end{aligned}
   $$
3. Tentukan solusi dari $-4x + 6 \geq 2$.
   **Jawab:** $$
   \begin{aligned}
	 -4x + 6 &\geq 2 \\
	 -4x &\geq 2 - 6 \\
	 -4x &\geq -3 \\
	 x &\geq \frac{-3}{-4} \\
	 x &\geq 0.75
   \end{aligned}
   $$
4. Selesaikan $x-3\leq7$ dan gambarkan solusinya pada garis bilangan.
   **Jawab:** $$
   \begin{aligned}
	 x - 3 &\leq 7 \\
	 x &\leq 7 + 3 \\
	 x &\leq 10
   \end{aligned}
   $$

5. Jika $5x - 10 < 0$, tentukan interval nilai $x$.
   **Jawab:** $$
   \begin{aligned}
 5x - 10 &< 0 \\
 5x &< 0 + 10 \\
 5x &< 10 \\
 x &< \frac{10}{5} \\
 x &< 2  
   \end{aligned}
   $$
