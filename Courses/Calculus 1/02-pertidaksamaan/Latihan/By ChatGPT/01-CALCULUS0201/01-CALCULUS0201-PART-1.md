#### Latihan 1: Pertidaksamaan Linear

1. Selesaikan $2x - 5 > 9$.
   **Jawab:** $$
   \begin{aligned}
   2x - 5 &> 9 \\
   2x &> 9 + 5 \\
   2x &> 14 \\
   x &> \frac{14}{2} \\
   x &> 7
   \end{aligned}
   $$
2. Selesaikan $3x + 4 \leq 19$.
   **Jawab:** $$
   \begin{aligned}
	 3x + 4 &\leq 19 \\
	 3x &\leq 19 - 4 \\
	 3x &\leq 15 \\
	 x &\leq \frac{15}{3} \\
	 x \leq 5
   \end{aligned}
   $$
3. Tentukan solusi dari $-4x + 6 \geq 2$.
   **Jawab:** $$
   \begin{aligned}
	 -4x + 6 &\geq 2 \\
	 -4x &\geq 2 - 6 \\
	 -4x &\geq -3 \\
	 x &\geq \frac{-3}{-4} \\
	 x &\geq 0.75
   \end{aligned}
   $$
4. Selesaikan $x-3\leq7$ dan gambarkan solusinya pada garis bilangan.
   **Jawab:** $$
   \begin{aligned}
	 x - 3 &\leq 7 \\
	 x &\leq 7 + 3 \\
	 x &\leq 10
   \end{aligned}
   $$
   Garis bilangan $x \leq 10$

5. Jika $5x - 10 < 0$, tentukan interval nilai $x$.
   **Jawab:** $$
   \begin{aligned}
 5x - 10 &< 0 \\
 5x &< 0 + 10 \\
 5x &< 10 \\
 x &< \frac{10}{5} \\
 \therefore x &< 2  
   \end{aligned}
   $$

#### Latihan 2: Pertidaksamaan dengan Bilangan Negatif

1. Selesaikan $-2x + 3 > 7$.
   **Jawab:**$$\begin{aligned}
   -2x + 3 &> 7 \\
   -2x &> 7 - 3 \\
   -2x &> 4 \\
   x &< \frac{4}{-2} \\
   \therefore x &< -2
   \end{aligned}
   $$
2. Tentukan solusi dari $-5x-8 \geq 2$.
   **Jawab:**$$\begin{aligned}
   -5x - 8 &\geq 2 \\
   -5x &\geq 2 + 8 \\
   -5x &\geq 10 \\
   x &\leq \frac{10}{-5} \\
   \therefore x &\leq -2 
   \end{aligned}
   $$
3. Jika $-6x + 10 < 4$, berapa nilai $x$?
   **Jawab:**$$\begin{aligned}
   -6x + 10 &< 4 \\
   -6x &< 4 - 10 \\
   -6x &< 6 \\
   x &> \frac{6}{-6} \\
   \therefore x &> -1 
   \end{aligned}
   $$
4. Selesaikan $-3x-5 \leq -11$.
   **Jawab:**$$\begin{aligned}
   -3x - 5 &\leq -11 \\
   -3x &\leq -11 + 5 \\
   -3x &\leq -6 \\
   x &\leq \frac{-6}{-3} \\
   \therefore x &\leq 2
   \end{aligned}
   $$
5. Tentukan $x$ untuk $-7x+9 > 16$.
   **Jawab:**$$\begin{aligned}
   -7x + 9 &> 16 \\
   -7x &> 16 - 9 \\
   -7x &> 7 \\
   x &< \frac{7}{-7} \\
   \therefore x &< -1
   \end{aligned}
	$$
